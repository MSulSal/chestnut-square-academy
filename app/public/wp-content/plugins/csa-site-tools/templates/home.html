Your Most Asked Questions

What will my child be learning?

Focused on six key outcomes for your child, our proprietary, developmentally-appropriate Life Essentials curriculum gives your child a full day of fun and learning.

What are your teacher qualifications and training requirements?

All Chestnut Square Academy educators must meet or exceed the state requirements for child care providers, including background clearance, education qualifications, and ongoing professional development.

How much is tuition at Chestnut Square Academy ?

Tuition at Chestnut Square Academy is affected by your child’s age, programs, days and hours your child attends, and location. Contact your local Academy for specific costs.

What if my child has food allergies?

The health and safety of your child is our top priority. Please, speak with your local Chestnut Square Academy directly to learn how we will accommodate your family.

How do you use technology in the classroom?

Technology is incorporated as a supportive learning tool in a balanced way for learning activities such as science, literacy, and math content.

How can I find out more about Chestnut Square Academy?

Chestnut Square Academy has been shaping, fueling, and nurturing children’s natural curiosity since we opened over 40 years ago. You can find out more by visiting the Our Company page.

What is your health and safety policy?

Each Chestnut Square Academy has secure entries, authorized pick-up procedures, cleaning protocols, and completes background checks for all staff. To keep children healthy, Chestnut Square Academy follows our Health EssentialsSM guidelines to uphold the highest level of health precautions and cleanliness.
