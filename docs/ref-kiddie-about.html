Community Begins Here®

Making the Most of Every Learning Moment, from Day One

We’ve been shaping, fueling, and nurturing children’s natural curiosity since we opened our first Academy over 40 years ago. We’re driven to prepare children for life. Through our passion for early childhood education, community commitment, and our Life Essentials® curriculum, we’re here to educate and encourage your child to do more and be more—not just while they’re with us, but outside the classroom as well.

Explore Our History

The Kiddie Academy Difference

Care and curriculum go hand in hand. We nurture and guide children, inspiring them to develop a life-long love of learning. Our proprietary curriculum, Life Essentials, was crafted to focus on six key outcomes, preparing your child for life.

Our Commitment

We are a community that nurtures, educates, and inspires children for the future in Kiddie Academy locations across the country.

Family Owned

Founded in 1981, George and Pauline Miller combined their passion to create an educational child care brand that creates lasting memories. The Miller family continues to own and operate Kiddie Academy to this day. Over the years, they have empowered other families to own and operate Academies in their communities.

Empowering Children

We empower children to actively explore their curiosity and spark a desire to learn.

Our Education Experts

A Curriculum with Purpose

A perfect blend of nurturing and education is vital for young children. Our education experts have a deep understanding of childhood development and psychology and continuously enhance Life Essentials to best meet the needs of your child.

Setting High Standards

Focus on Excellence

We set high standards and deliver exceptional educational experiences. The quality of our curriculum, focus on benchmarking measures of excellence, and proactively securing accreditation are just a few ways we achieve this.
